Mo:Mo

Mo:Mo is a traditional Nepali dish enjoyed across the country. It's popularity has also spread outside home. It is similar to dumplings but has a Nepali twist.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Onions
  • Ground chicken
  • Mo:mo masala

Recipe

  1. Knead dough from flour.
  2. Prepare fillings for Mo:mos using masala, ground chicken and onions to taste.
  3. Wrap the filling with the dough prepared beforehand.
  4. Let Mo:mos cook on steam
