Bhutti Mam

Fried rice but with a Nepali twist. Bhutti Mam literally translates to fried rice.

Ingredients

  • Leftover rice
  • Meat of choice
  • Vegetable of choice
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Fry protein and vegetable of choice in high heat.
  2. Add leftover rice to the mix.
  3. Add soy sauce to enhance taste.
  4. Add garnish on top.
